The Different Types of Clocks

Mechanical
A mechanical clock is made from simple mechanical components (not electrical). It is usually powered by a coiled spring or falling weights.
Electric Clock
As the name suggests, an electric clock works on electricity that is supplied through a battery or other power source.

Atomic clocks are calibrated against the frequency of a resonating atom, usually cesium.
